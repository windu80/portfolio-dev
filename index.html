WAsP - Wind Resource Assessment softwareOverview

Being one of the main components of WindPRO, the WAsP software is the industry standard for wind resource assessment, siting and energy yield calculation for wind turbines and wind farms. Created by the Technical University of Denmark (DTU), it is also the basis for the Global Wind Atlas.

Portfolio WAsP

I realized a few Case Analyses, studying the wind resource in different areas, and working out the Predicted Wind Climate, taking the terrain (roughness and elevation) into account.

Geographic Information System (GIS)Overview

Being adept in GIS means knowing the concepts, tools, and techniques to make maps that answer geographic questions, i.e. knowing how to create GIS data, how to find and evaluate data found online, how to design an effective map, and how to filter data and analyze spatial relationships, and how to work with satellite imagery.

For instance, we can analyze maps of wind or solar resource and by constraint analysis, with factors such as distance from transmission lines or proximity from agglomerations, we can determine the best areas suitable for a new Wind or Solar Farm.
